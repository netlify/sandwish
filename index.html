    Sandwish

    The world must know about that unique combination of sandwich ingredients you love.

    To create your sandwish:

    1. Click "New"
    2. Click "Add layer" and start adding ingredients
    3. Set your name and the name of your sandwish
    4. Click "Save" to get a shareable link

    This site is built with Vite, the Netlify Vite Plugin, Netlify Functions and Netlify Blobs.
